The goal of this project is to produce a poster that you can present at UCF research week (https://researchweek.ucf.edu/). You are not required to participate in order to get credit for this class, but I highly encourage you to participate to gain practice and pad your resume. 

For this project you will locate a data set or merge multiple data sets that are related to bio statistics in order to answer a research question of your choosing. 

Your poster needs to include: 

  • Title and name of group members
  • Introductions (3-4 sentences introducing the topic, citing relevant studies, etc.)
  • Research question and hypothesis (1 sentence each)
  • Description of your data (how did you get it, what does it contain, could use summary graphics, etc.)
  • Analysis 
  • Conditions/Assumptions (can be put under analysis)
  • Conclusion
  • References (for your introduction and data)

You will need to upload the poster as well as an audio recording of you (as a group) talking about your poster. This recording should be between 4 and 5 minutes long and would be your quick explanation of your study. Due 11/21. 

To receive full credit for this assignment you will also need to provide peer feedback to two other groups. This should not take long and should follow the attached rubric. Due 11/27.

The posters need to be uploaded both under both this assignment and Research posters and comments. You need to provide your feedback at Research posters and comments.
